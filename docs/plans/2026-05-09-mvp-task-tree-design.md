# Timestream Chronicles: MVP Vertical Slice (Totter's Lane)

## 📌 Objective
To establish a playable "Vertical Slice" for the MVP that proves the core incremental loop: performing tasks, managing Entropy, resetting the timeline (Prestige), and utilizing inventory to survive longer. The narrative covers the events of *An Unearthly Child* (1963).

## ⚙️ Core Mechanics Required
1. **Task Unlock Logic:** Tasks must remain hidden or unselectable until prerequisites (Skill Levels, Item Inventory, or previous Task completions) are met.
2. **Inventory Protocol:** The ability to gather items (e.g., "Raw Energy") and consume them to restore Stability.
3. **Entropy Weighting:** Tasks must have varying Entropy drain rates to force strategic decision-making.

---

## 🗺️ The Task Progression Tree

### Phase 1: Investigation (Early Game)
*Goal: Establish the baseline loop and introduce the core skills before the first timeline collapse.*

1. **Lurk in Shadows**
   - **Skill:** Stealth
   - **Action:** Hide behind the scrap piles.
   - **Metrics:** Low XP, Very Low Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Available immediately.

2. **Navigate the Junk Maze**
   - **Skill:** Stealth
   - **Action:** Learn the layout of the junkyard without making noise.
   - **Metrics:** Moderate XP, Moderate Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Stealth Level 2.

3. **Observe the Teachers**
   - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
   - **Action:** Analyze Ian and Barbara from afar. The "bridge" task to the Science skill.
   - **Metrics:** Moderate XP, Low Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Stealth Level 3.

4. **Analyze the Police Box**
   - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
   - **Action:** Begin basic diagnostics on the exterior of the TARDIS.
   - **Metrics:** High XP, High Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Science Level 2.

5. **Scavenge Scrap Metal**
   - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
   - **Action:** Gather mundane materials needed to patch the TARDIS fluid link.
   - **Metrics:** Moderate XP, Moderate Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Science Level 3.

---

### Phase 2: Assembly & Survival (Mid-Game)
*Goal: Introduce the Inventory Protocol. The player must actively manage their Stability to survive longer loops.*

6. **Distract the Policeman**
   - **Skill:** Stealth
   - **Action:** A policeman is patrolling outside the gates. Create a diversion.
   - **Metrics:** High XP, Moderate Entropy.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Stealth Level 5.

7. **Siphon Local Power Grid**
   - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
   - **Action:** Tap into the 1963 London power grid.
   - **Reward:** Drops **"Raw Energy"** into the player's inventory periodically.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Science Level 4, Stealth Level 5.

8. **Calibrate the Fluid Link**
   - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
   - **Action:** Repair the damaged fluid link using scavenged metal.
   - **Metrics:** Massive total progress required. Impossible without high Mastery.
   - **Unlock:** Requires Science Level 6.

9. **Purify Artron Cells** *(Instant Action)*
   - **Action:** Consumes "Raw Energy" to restore a chunk of **Stability**.
   - **Unlock:** Available once "Siphon Local Power Grid" is unlocked.

---

### Phase 3: The Escape (End-Game)
*Goal: The climax. A brutal balancing act of high-drain tasks and constant healing to reach the Win Condition.*

10. **Confront the Teachers**
    - **Skill:** Stealth (Deception/Deflection)
    - **Action:** Ian and Barbara have forced their way inside. Keep them distracted while you prepare the ship.
    - **Metrics:** Extreme Entropy drain.
    - **Unlock:** Requires Science Level 8, Stealth Level 8.

11. **Override the Faulty Nav-Com**
    - **Skill:** Scientific Inquiry
    - **Action:** The MK I computer is refusing to fly. Force a manual override on the isomorphic controls.
    - **Metrics:** Massive progress requirement, High Entropy drain.
    - **Unlock:** Requires "Confront the Teachers" complete.

12. **Emergency Dematerialization** *(Win Condition)*
    - **Action:** A short, massively draining task ("Kidnapping" the teachers and fleeing). If the player survives to 100%, the timeline advances.
    - **Reward:** Unlocks the "Prehistoric Earth" era and grants a massive permanent boost to Base Stability (a "Temporal Anchor").
    - **Unlock:** Requires "Override the Faulty Nav-Com" complete.
