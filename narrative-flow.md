# Narrative Flow: Timestream Chronicles

## Era 1: The First Doctor (William Hartnell - 1963)
**Core Theme:** The Reluctant Traveler. The TARDIS is unpredictable, and the Doctor is learning to survive hostile environments while discovering the true nature of his ship.

### Chapter 1: 76 Totter's Lane
* **Setting:** A foggy junkyard in 1963 London.
* **The Threat (Stability Decay):** Suspicion. Teachers Ian and Barbara are investigating. If Stability hits 0, they involve the authorities and the timeline collapses.
* **Tasks:**
    * `Lurk in Shadows` (Skill: Stealth) - *Low Entropy Weight. Safe, slow progress.*
    * `Analyze Junkyard` (Skill: Scientific Inquiry) - *Medium Entropy Weight.*
    * `Eavesdrop on Teachers` (Skill: Deduction) - *High Entropy Weight. Fast XP, but drains Stability quickly.*
* **The Gate (Unlock):** Reaching Focus Level 5 in *Stealth* unlocks the "Enter the TARDIS" task.
* **Chapter Milestone:** Entering the TARDIS secures progress and shifts the location.

### Chapter 2: The First Flight
* **Setting:** Inside the TARDIS Console Room.
* **The Threat:** System failure. If Stability hits 0, the dematerialization circuit blows.
* **Tasks:**
    * `Study the Console` (Skill: Temporal Engineering)
    * `Reroute Power` (Skill: Temporal Engineering) - *Yields "Artron Cells" (Inventory items that restore Stability).*
    * `Initiate Dematerialization` (Skill: Piloting) - *Requires Focus Level 10 Engineering and consumes Artron Cells.*
* **Narrative Anchor (Milestone):** Successful Dematerialization. 
    * **Reward:** Permanently halves `baseDecay` (representing the Doctor successfully lifting off).

### Chapter 3: The Tribe of Gum (100,000 BC)
* **Setting:** Prehistoric Earth.
* **The Threat:** The freezing cold and hostile cavemen.
* **Tasks:**
    * `Gather Kindling` (Skill: Survival)
    * `Observe Tribe` (Skill: Stealth)
    * `Create Fire` (Skill: Survival) - *A high-entropy task requiring massive Survival Mastery built up in previous loops to survive the attempt.*
* **Chapter Milestone:** Escaping the tribe and returning to the TARDIS.

### Chapter 4: The Dead Planet (Skaro)
* **Setting:** The petrified jungles and metallic cities of Skaro.
* **The Threat:** Radiation sickness and the Daleks.
* **Tasks:**
    * `Explore Petrified Jungle` (Skill: Survival) - *Yields "Anti-Radiation Drugs" (Stabilization item).*
    * `Navigate Dalek City` (Skill: Stealth & Deduction)
    * `Recover Fluid Link` (Skill: Temporal Engineering)
* **Era Transition (Regeneration):** Defeating the Daleks and escaping Skaro marks the end of the Hartnell Era. 
    * **Reward:** The Doctor regenerates into the Second Doctor (Troughton). This unlocks new fundamental skills, changes the UI aesthetic slightly, and introduces new mechanics (e.g., manipulation over observation).
